PICT B.E. Course Structure

First Year (FE)

  • Mathematics I
    Limits, derivatives & integrations
  • Physics
    Mechanics & properties of matter
  • Chemistry
    Organic & inorganic basics
  • Workshop Practice
    Basic engineering tools & techniques

Second Year (SE)

  • Data Structures
    Arrays, lists, trees & graphs
  • Discrete Mathematics
    Logic, sets & combinatorics
  • OOP with Java
    Classes, objects & inheritance
  • Digital Logic
    Gates, flip-flops & state machines

Third Year (TE)

  • Microprocessors
    8085 architecture & programming
  • Database Systems
    ER-models, SQL & normalization
  • Operating Systems
    Processes, scheduling & memory
  • Computer Networks
    OSI model & TCP/IP basics

Final Year (BE)

  • Software Engineering
    SDLC & design patterns
  • Artificial Intelligence
    Search, learning & reasoning
  • Elective I
    Choose advanced topic
  • Final Project
    Team-based capstone project
